# Privacy Policy for CardioTrack

**Last Updated: February 9, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to CardioTrack. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your data.

## Data Collection and Storage

### Local Storage Only
CardioTrack stores all your health data **locally on your device only**. We do not:
- Collect any personal information
- Transmit your health data to external servers
- Share your data with third parties
- Track your usage or behavior

### Data We Store Locally
The app stores the following information on your device:
- Blood pressure measurements (systolic and diastolic values)
- Timestamps of measurements
- Reminder preferences
- Notes associated with measurements

### Biometric Authentication
If you choose to use Face ID or Touch ID:
- Biometric data is processed by your device's secure enclave
- We never access, store, or transmit your biometric information
- This feature is entirely optional

## Notifications
If you enable reminders:
- Local notifications are scheduled on your device
- No data is sent to external notification services
- You can disable notifications at any time in app settings

## Data Security
Your health data is secured by:
- Device-level encryption (iOS secure storage)
- Optional biometric authentication
- No network transmission of health data

## Your Rights
You have complete control over your data:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- Deleting the app removes all stored data
- You can export or delete data at any time within the app

## Children's Privacy
CardioTrack does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is intended for adults monitoring their blood pressure.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Third-Party Services
CardioTrack uses the following third-party services:
- Expo framework for app infrastructure (no data collection)
- Local device storage (AsyncStorage)

## Medical Disclaimer
CardioTrack is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult with a healthcare professional for medical advice. In case of medical emergency, immediately contact emergency services.

## Data Retention
- Data is retained on your device until you manually delete it
- Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data
- We do not have access to retrieve deleted data

## International Users
Since all data is stored locally on your device, no international data transfers occur.

## GDPR Compliance (EU Users)
For users in the European Union:
- Right to access: Access your data within the app
- Right to rectification: Edit your data within the app
- Right to erasure: Delete data within the app or by uninstalling
- Right to data portability: Export feature available in app
- No automated decision-making or profiling occurs

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)
For California residents:
- We do not sell your personal information
- We do not collect personal information beyond what's stored locally
- You have the right to delete your data (by deleting the app)
