# CardioTrack Support

Welcome to CardioTrack support! We're here to help you get the most out of your blood pressure tracking experience.

## Quick Start Guide

### Getting Started
1. **First Launch**: Open the app and set up biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) for secure access
2. **Add Your First Measurement**: Tap the "Add Measurement" button on the home screen
3. **Set Reminders**: Enable reminders to help you track your blood pressure regularly
4. **View History**: Check your blood pressure trends in the Calendar or History sections

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is CardioTrack a medical device?**  
A: No, CardioTrack is not a medical device. It's a tracking tool to help you monitor your blood pressure. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**  
A: All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not send your health information to any external servers.

**Q: What if I lose my device?**  
A: Since data is stored locally, losing your device means losing your data. We recommend regularly taking screenshots or exporting your data for backup purposes.

**Q: Can I use this app on multiple devices?**  
A: Each installation maintains its own local data. Data does not automatically sync between devices.

### Features

**Q: How do I record a blood pressure measurement?**  
A: Tap "Add Measurement" on the home screen, enter your systolic and diastolic values, and save.

**Q: Can I edit or delete measurements?**  
A: Yes, navigate to your measurement history and tap on any entry to edit or delete it.

**Q: How do reminders work?**  
A: Enable reminders when adding a measurement. The app will send local notifications at your chosen times.

**Q: What do the colors mean?**  
A: Blood pressure readings are color-coded based on standard medical guidelines:
- Green: Normal range
- Yellow: Elevated
- Orange: High (Stage 1 Hypertension)
- Red: Very high (Stage 2 Hypertension or Hypertensive Crisis)

**IMPORTANT**: These are general guidelines. Always follow your doctor's specific recommendations.

### Technical Support

**Q: The app won't open or crashes**  
A: Try these steps:
1. Restart your device
2. Ensure you have the latest iOS version
3. Reinstall the app (Note: This will delete your local data)
4. Contact support if the issue persists

**Q: Notifications aren't working**  
A: Check that:
1. Notifications are enabled in iOS Settings > CardioTrack
2. Do Not Disturb mode is not active
3. Reminders are properly configured in the app

**Q: Face ID/Touch ID not working**  
A: Ensure:
1. Biometric authentication is set up in iOS Settings
2. You've granted the app permission to use Face ID/Touch ID
3. Your device supports biometric authentication

**Q: Data export not working**  
A: Contact support with details about the issue. We're continuously improving this feature.

## Understanding Blood Pressure

### Normal Blood Pressure Ranges (Adult)
- **Normal**: Systolic < 120 and Diastolic < 80
- **Elevated**: Systolic 120-129 and Diastolic < 80
- **Stage 1 Hypertension**: Systolic 130-139 or Diastolic 80-89
- **Stage 2 Hypertension**: Systolic ≥ 140 or Diastolic ≥ 90
- **Hypertensive Crisis**: Systolic > 180 and/or Diastolic > 120

**IMPORTANT**: These are general guidelines from the American Heart Association. Your healthcare provider may have different target ranges for you based on your individual health conditions.

### When to Seek Medical Attention
Seek immediate medical care if you experience:
- Blood pressure readings consistently above 180/120
- Severe headache
- Chest pain
- Shortness of breath
- Vision changes
- Difficulty speaking
- Numbness or weakness

## Best Practices

1. **Consistency**: Measure at the same time each day
2. **Proper Technique**: 
   - Rest for 5 minutes before measuring
   - Sit with back supported and feet flat
   - Keep arm at heart level
   - Don't talk during measurement
3. **Regular Monitoring**: Track measurements as recommended by your doctor
4. **Share with Doctor**: Show your tracking history to your healthcare provider
